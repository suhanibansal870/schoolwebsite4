Campus Life

multipurpose room

Was established in 1999

Sandpier School has a multipurpose room. This room is used to pick up lunch, dance, play games, have parties (rarely), listen to announcments, and more. Without this room, the school wouldn't be half as fun as it is now. This room has many uses and it is really helpful.

school library

Was established in 1997

This is a library where students can enjoy reading books. The library is filled with books and stories kids can read. Kids also have a comfortable place for sitting and reading their books. This library is one of the best places in the school.

school field

Was established in 2000

The school field is for kids to play sports. It is mainly used for Physical Education. Sometimes, it is used to play football, soccer, and other sports. Kids also use it for the running competition in the school.
